// the muse

Meet your
running muse.

Fitness has always been my anchor, but for me it's never just been about the heavy lifts in the gym — it's about the energy you share outside of it. My Muscle Muse started as a personal space to document that journey, but it quickly became clear that the best part of fitness is the community.

Having just moved back to Hyderabad this July, the best way I know how to reconnect with the city is by hitting the pavement. I wanted to take the "Let's crush goals together" mindset off the screen and bring it to the track. M³RC was born out of a desire to build a local fitness family from the ground up. Whether you're lacing up for the very first time or chasing a personal record, this space is yours. No egos, no drop pace. Just kilometres and good people.

The goal is simple: build a crew where everyone feels strong enough to show up — and proud when they do.

— Divyani
